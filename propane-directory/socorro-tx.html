Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Socorro, TX

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Socorro, TX?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity — you only pay for the gas added. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled replacement, but exchange tanks are typically filled to only 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is usually the more economical choice when a refill station is nearby.

How much does propane cost in Socorro, TX?

Propane prices in Socorro, TX typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon for retail refills. Prices fluctuate with the energy market and season — winter months often see higher demand. Call ahead to confirm the current rate before making the drive.

Where can I exchange a propane tank in Socorro, TX?

Propane exchange tanks (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) are available at hardware stores and fuel stops in Socorro and the El Paso Valley. Listings marked "Exchange" on this page offer this service. No appointment needed — bring your empty tank and swap it for a pre-filled one.

Can I get large propane tanks filled near Socorro, TX?

Yes. Several suppliers in Socorro and the El Paso Valley can fill larger tanks (100 lb and above). Contact the supplier directly to confirm tank size limits and whether an appointment is required. Some locations also offer delivery to El Paso and the lower Rio Grande valley and surrounding areas.
