What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Arlington?

A propane refill tops off your existing cylinder to its full capacity — you pay only for the gas used. A propane exchange swaps your empty cylinder for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks typically contain only about 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is typically the better value when a refill station is available nearby.
