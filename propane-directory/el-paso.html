About Propane Services in El Paso

El Paso sits at the westernmost tip of Texas on the border with New Mexico and Mexico, making it one of the most geographically isolated major Texas cities. Propane is an important fuel source here for both residential and commercial customers, particularly in the outlying communities of the Franklin Mountains foothills and the Upper Valley. The high desert climate means cold winters where propane heating systems see regular use.

El Paso's border location means some suppliers also serve customers in southern New Mexico. If you're traveling through far West Texas, El Paso is the main hub for propane services in the region — the next major city (San Antonio) is over 550 miles east.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in El Paso

Where can I refill my propane tank in El Paso?

El Paso has propane refill and exchange locations at major hardware stores, grocery stores, and dedicated propane suppliers across the city. Use the listings above to find the nearest location with addresses and phone numbers.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in El Paso?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full 20-lb capacity — you pay only for the propane used. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks typically contain only about 15 lbs of gas. Refill is generally the better deal when a refill station is nearby.

How much does propane cost in El Paso?

Propane prices in El Paso typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon, though prices vary by supplier and season. Winter months often see price increases due to higher heating demand. Call ahead to confirm current pricing before visiting.

Do El Paso propane suppliers serve New Mexico residents?

Many El Paso-area propane suppliers serve customers in the greater El Paso–Las Cruces metro region, including southern New Mexico. Contact individual suppliers to confirm their service area and whether they offer delivery to your location.
