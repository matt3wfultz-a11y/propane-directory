About Propane Services in Las Cruces

Las Cruces is New Mexico's second-largest city, located in the Mesilla Valley along the Rio Grande near the Texas and Mexico borders. The city's Chihuahuan Desert climate brings hot summers and mild but occasionally cold winters, making propane a common fuel for home heating, outdoor cooking, and agricultural operations in the surrounding Doña Ana County. Las Cruces is also home to New Mexico State University, whose research farms and extension service add to the area's agricultural propane demand.

The Las Cruces area is closely connected to the El Paso, TX metro — residents on both sides of the state line frequently use suppliers in either city. Several Las Cruces propane dealers serve rural Doña Ana County communities including Mesilla, Anthony, Chaparral, and the Hatch Valley chili farming region, where propane is essential for crop drying operations.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Las Cruces?

A propane refill fills your existing cylinder to its full capacity — you pay only for what goes in. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks are typically filled to only 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is the better value when a refill station is accessible.

How much does propane cost in Las Cruces?

Propane prices in Las Cruces typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon, similar to neighboring El Paso, TX due to shared regional distribution infrastructure. Call ahead to confirm current pricing before your visit.
