Alamogordo sits in the Tularosa Basin at the foot of the Sacramento Mountains, with cold winters and a strong propane culture for both heating and outdoor cooking. Whether you're a local resident, an RV visitor to White Sands, or a camper heading into Lincoln National Forest, the listings below cover your propane needs.

Alamogordo Propane Locations

About Propane Services in Alamogordo, NM

Alamogordo is a mid-sized city in southern New Mexico known for White Sands National Park and Holloman Air Force Base. The high desert climate brings cold winters and cool nights year-round, making propane a popular fuel for home heating, water heaters, and outdoor cooking. The city has several dedicated propane suppliers as well as exchange kiosks at major retailers along White Sands Blvd.

Visitors to White Sands and campers in Lincoln National Forest often stock up on propane in Alamogordo before heading into more remote areas. Local refill stations can fill both standard grill tanks and the larger cylinders used in RVs and camper vans.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Alamogordo

Is Alamogordo a good place to fill propane before visiting White Sands or Lincoln National Forest?

Yes — Alamogordo is the practical last stop for propane before heading into remote areas. White Sands National Park has no propane services on-site, and Lincoln National Forest campgrounds similarly lack fueling options. Whether you're filling a camp stove canister, a standard 20-lb grill cylinder, or an RV coach tank, stocking up in Alamogordo before heading west to White Sands or east into the Sacramento Mountains is strongly recommended. Local suppliers are accustomed to serving campers and RV visitors and can fill most tank sizes without advance notice.

How much does propane cost in Alamogordo, NM?

Retail propane refill prices in Alamogordo generally run between $3.25 and $4.75 per gallon. Because Alamogordo sits at the end of a distribution spur rather than on a major interstate supply route, prices here tend to run slightly higher than in Las Cruces or El Paso. Prices rise further in fall and winter when residential heating demand increases across the Tularosa Basin. Calling the supplier directly before visiting is the fastest way to confirm the current per-gallon rate.

Can I fill an RV or motorhome propane tank in Alamogordo?

Yes. Several propane suppliers in Alamogordo can fill ASME-stamped RV coach tanks — the permanently mounted tanks that exchange programs cannot accept. This matters because many travelers stopping near White Sands are in Class A motorhomes or fifth wheels with large onboard propane systems. Call ahead to confirm that the specific supplier has a pull-through accessible fill station if you're driving a large rig, and ask whether they can accommodate same-day fills without an appointment.

Where can I exchange a propane tank along White Sands Blvd in Alamogordo?

Exchange cages (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) are available at hardware retailers and grocery stores along White Sands Boulevard (US-70), Alamogordo's main commercial corridor. These locations are the most convenient for a quick in-and-out swap. Use the "Exchange" filter in the listings above to find specific locations. No appointment is needed — just bring your empty cylinder and pay at the register. For better per-gallon value, refill stations in Alamogordo fill to the full 20-lb capacity rather than the 15-lb partial fills standard at exchange kiosks.

Do Alamogordo propane suppliers serve Cloudcroft or Ruidoso?

Some suppliers in Alamogordo deliver to the Sacramento Mountain communities of Cloudcroft and Ruidoso, where propane is a primary heating fuel and natural gas service is limited. Cloudcroft sits at 8,600 feet elevation and Ruidoso at around 6,900 feet — both experience significantly colder winters than Alamogordo itself, driving strong residential propane demand. Contact suppliers directly to ask about delivery service area, minimum fill amounts, and seasonal scheduling for mountain deliveries.
