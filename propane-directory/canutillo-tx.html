About Propane Services in Canutillo, TX

Canutillo sits in the Upper Valley of El Paso County along Doniphan Drive and the Rio Grande. The area is largely residential and agricultural, with propane used widely for grills, outdoor heaters, and farm equipment. Local suppliers serve both small cylinders and larger tank deliveries.

For the quickest access, exchange kiosks along Doniphan Drive offer tank swaps without appointments. Refill stations provide better value per gallon for customers who own their tanks.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Canutillo

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Canutillo?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity — you only pay for the gas added. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled replacement, but exchange tanks are typically filled to only 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is usually the more economical choice when a refill station is nearby.

How much does propane cost in Canutillo?

Propane prices in Canutillo typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon for retail refills. Prices fluctuate with the energy market and season — winter months often see higher demand. Call ahead to confirm the current rate before making the drive.

Where can I exchange a propane tank in Canutillo?

Propane exchange tanks (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) are available at hardware stores and fuel stops in Canutillo and the Upper Valley. Listings marked "Exchange" on this page offer this service. No appointment needed — bring your empty tank and swap it for a pre-filled one.

Can I get large propane tanks filled near Canutillo?

Yes. Several suppliers in Canutillo and the Upper Valley can fill larger tanks (100 lb and above). Contact the supplier directly to confirm tank size limits and whether an appointment is required. Some locations also offer delivery to El Paso and the Upper Valley and surrounding areas.
