About Propane Services in Santa Fe

Santa Fe is New Mexico's capital and one of the highest-elevation major cities in the United States, sitting at approximately 7,000 feet above sea level. The combination of altitude and northern latitude means Santa Fe experiences real winters — cold enough that propane heating is essential for many homes, especially in the surrounding foothills and the communities of Tesuque, Galisteo, and the Española Valley. Many Santa Fe area homes were built without natural gas connections and rely entirely on propane for heating, cooking, and hot water.

The Santa Fe area's artistic and tourism economy also drives demand for propane for outdoor event heaters, food truck operations, and patio dining setups year-round. Local propane dealers serve both the city proper and the broader Santa Fe County region.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Santa Fe

Where can I refill my propane tank in Santa Fe?

Santa Fe has propane refill and exchange locations at hardware stores and dedicated propane dealers throughout the city and surrounding Santa Fe County. Use the listings above to find the nearest location with addresses and phone numbers.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Santa Fe?

A propane refill fills your existing cylinder to its full capacity — you pay only for the gas used. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks typically contain only about 15 lbs instead of the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is the better value when a station is nearby.

How much does propane cost in Santa Fe?

Propane prices in Santa Fe typically range from $3.50 to $5.00 per gallon — slightly higher than larger cities due to the smaller local market and delivery costs. Call ahead to confirm current pricing at your preferred supplier.

Do Santa Fe propane suppliers offer delivery to rural areas?

Yes — several Santa Fe-area propane dealers offer delivery to surrounding communities including Tesuque, Galisteo, Lamy, Pecos, and the Española Valley. Contact suppliers directly to confirm delivery range and scheduling for residential and agricultural accounts.
