Searching for propane near El Paso, TX? Whether you need a refill for your grill, a new propane tank, a swap at an exchange cage, or bulk delivery for home heating, El Paso has you covered. Types of Propane Services Near El Paso

When you search "propane near me," results can mean several different things. Here's how the main service types break down in El Paso:

  • Propane refill stations: Bring your empty cylinder and an attendant fills it to capacity. Best value — you pay only for what goes in, at lower per-gallon rates than exchange. Available at dealers, U-Haul locations, and select hardware stores.
  • Propane tank exchange: Swap an empty cylinder for a pre-filled one at a self-serve cage. Available 24/7 at Walmart, Home Depot, grocery stores, and most El Paso gas stations. Convenient but costs more per gallon, and tanks are only filled to 15 lbs (not the full 20-lb capacity).
  • Propane tank sales: Need a new cylinder? Hardware stores, propane dealers, and some big-box retailers sell new tanks — typically a 20-lb grill cylinder runs $35–$55 empty.
  • Bulk propane delivery: For home heating, large agricultural use, or permanent tanks (100-gallon and above), a propane company delivers directly to your tank. Most El Paso-area dealers offer this service.

Where to Buy a Propane Tank in El Paso

If you need a new cylinder — either because you don't own one or your existing tank failed recertification — here's where to buy one near El Paso:

  • Home Depot and Lowe's: Carry 20-lb empty cylinders (typically $35–$45). Also available pre-filled via exchange programs.
  • Walmart: Sells new tanks in the sporting goods or outdoor section, usually $35–$50.
  • Local propane dealers: Often sell new cylinders in all sizes — 20 lb, 33 lb, 40 lb, and 100 lb — and can fill them on the spot.
  • Tractor Supply: Good source for larger cylinders (33-lb, 40-lb) used for RVs and generators.
