About Propane Services in New Mexico

New Mexico is one of the most propane-dependent states in the American Southwest. With large rural areas, a cold high-desert climate, and limited natural gas infrastructure outside the major cities, propane serves as the primary heating and cooking fuel for hundreds of thousands of New Mexico households. From the Rio Grande valley to the remote communities of the Four Corners region, propane dealers and refill stations are essential local businesses.

New Mexico's three largest cities — Albuquerque, Santa Fe, and Las Cruces — each have established networks of propane suppliers serving both residential and commercial customers. The state's elevation (Albuquerque sits at 5,300 feet; Santa Fe at 7,000 feet) means winters are cold enough that propane heating season runs longer here than in neighboring Texas.
