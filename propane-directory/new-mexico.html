About Propane Services in New Mexico

New Mexico is one of the most propane-dependent states in the American Southwest. With large rural areas, a cold high-desert climate, and limited natural gas infrastructure outside the major cities, propane serves as the primary heating and cooking fuel for hundreds of thousands of New Mexico households. From the Rio Grande valley to the Tularosa Basin to the remote communities of the Four Corners region, propane dealers and refill stations are essential local businesses.

New Mexico's major cities — Albuquerque, Santa Fe, Las Cruces, Alamogordo, and Deming — each have established networks of propane suppliers. The state's elevation drives demand: Albuquerque sits at 5,300 feet, Santa Fe at 7,000 feet, and even southern cities like Alamogordo see cold winters that extend the heating season well beyond what neighboring Texas experiences.

Refill vs. Exchange in New Mexico

Exchange kiosks (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) are available at most major retailers in Albuquerque, Santa Fe, and Las Cruces. In smaller communities like Deming, Alamogordo, and Anthony, exchange cages are less common — dedicated local refill stations are often the primary option. Refill is almost always the better value: you pay per gallon for a full fill rather than a flat fee for a partially-filled exchange tank.

Propane Prices in New Mexico

Retail propane prices in New Mexico typically range from $3.25 to $5.00 per gallon, with prices higher in remote areas like Deming and the rural south than in Albuquerque. Heating demand peaks from November through March, when prices often rise. RV travelers passing through on I-10 and I-25 will find competitive pricing in Las Cruces and Albuquerque.

Propane for RVs and Campers

New Mexico is a top destination for RV travelers and campers, with White Sands, Carlsbad Caverns, and dozens of state parks drawing visitors year-round. Alamogordo and Deming are common resupply stops for travelers on I-10. Most refill stations in the directory can accommodate motorhome and travel trailer tanks — call ahead for large fills to confirm availability.
