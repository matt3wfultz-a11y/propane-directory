About Propane Services in Albuquerque

Albuquerque is New Mexico's largest city and the hub of the state's propane market. Sitting at 5,300 feet elevation in the high desert, the Duke City experiences cold winters where propane heating is essential for many households — particularly in the East Mountains communities of Tijeras, Edgewood, and Moriarty that lie outside the natural gas grid. Albuquerque's sprawling metro area, which includes Rio Rancho to the northwest, has a well-developed network of propane suppliers serving both residential and commercial customers.

The city's mix of suburban neighborhoods, commercial districts, and proximity to rural New Mexico means propane dealers here often stock a wide range of tank sizes, from standard 20-lb grill cylinders to bulk agricultural tanks. Many of the major national exchange brands (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) have strong exchange kiosk presence at Albuquerque grocery and hardware stores.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Albuquerque?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full rated capacity — you pay only for the gas you receive. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks are typically only filled to about 15 lbs instead of the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is generally the better value when a refill station is accessible.

Propane refill prices in Albuquerque typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon. New Mexico's proximity to major Southwest propane distribution infrastructure means prices are generally competitive. Call ahead to confirm current per-gallon pricing before your visit.

Do Albuquerque propane suppliers serve the East Mountains?

Yes — several Albuquerque-area propane dealers serve the East Mountains communities of Tijeras, Edgewood, Moriarty, and Estancia, where natural gas is unavailable and propane is the primary home heating fuel. Contact suppliers directly to confirm their delivery service area and scheduling.
