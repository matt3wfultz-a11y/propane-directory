About Propane Services in Fort Worth

Fort Worth is the cultural heart of the DFW Metroplex and home to a large residential and agricultural propane market. From the Stockyards district to suburban neighborhoods in Keller, Burleson, and Mansfield, propane is widely used for home heating, cooking, and outdoor grilling. Fort Worth's mix of urban and rural communities means many residents maintain larger residential tanks for whole-home propane systems alongside the standard 20-lb grill cylinders available at exchange kiosks.

Cowtown's proximity to rural Tarrant County also means several full-service propane dealers in the area handle bulk agricultural deliveries in addition to standard consumer refills. Call ahead to confirm which tank sizes a supplier can accommodate.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Fort Worth

Where can I refill my propane tank in Fort Worth?

Fort Worth has numerous propane refill stations at hardware stores, gas stations, and dedicated propane suppliers throughout Tarrant County. Use the listings above to find the nearest location with current contact information and ratings.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Fort Worth?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity — you pay only for the gas you receive. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks are usually only filled to about 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity, making refill the better value when available.

How much does propane cost in Fort Worth?

Propane refill prices in Fort Worth typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon depending on the supplier and market conditions. Prices rise during winter heating season. Calling ahead is the best way to confirm current per-gallon pricing.

Do Fort Worth propane suppliers offer large tank fills?

Yes — several Fort Worth area suppliers serve residential and agricultural customers with larger tank sizes (100 lb, 250-gallon, 500-gallon). Check the listing details above or call suppliers directly to confirm availability for larger fills.
