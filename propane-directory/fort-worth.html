What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Fort Worth?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity — you pay only for the gas you receive. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks are usually only filled to about 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity, making refill the better value when available.

How much does propane cost in Fort Worth?

Propane refill prices in Fort Worth typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon depending on the supplier and market conditions. Prices rise during winter heating season. Calling ahead is the best way to confirm current per-gallon pricing.

Do Fort Worth propane suppliers offer large tank fills?

Yes — several Fort Worth area suppliers serve residential and agricultural customers with larger tank sizes (100 lb, 250-gallon, 500-gallon). Check the listing details above or call suppliers directly to confirm availability for larger fills.
