Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FindMyPropane.com ("we," "our," or "the site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website. FindMyPropane.com is an independent propane refill and exchange directory. We do not sell products, require account registration, or collect payment information.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information automatically when you visit the site:

  • Log data: Your browser may transmit standard log information including your IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited. This data is used for security monitoring and aggregate traffic analysis.
  • Analytics data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are popular, how long visits last, and what searches bring people here. Google Analytics collects data anonymously and does not identify individual users.
  • Contact form submissions: If you use the contact form on our Contact page, your message is submitted via Google Forms. Google's privacy policy applies to that data.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily submit it through the contact form.

Business Directory Information

FindMyPropane.com displays publicly available business information including business names, addresses, phone numbers, and service types. This information is sourced from publicly accessible data and does not constitute personal information under applicable privacy laws. If you operate a business listed in our directory and wish to update or remove your listing, please contact us.

Advertising — Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites across the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Visiting AboutAds.info
  • Disabling third-party cookies in your browser settings

Google's Privacy Policy governs how Google uses data collected through AdSense. We do not control Google's data practices. FindMyPropane.com does not use ad revenue to prioritize, sponsor, or influence business listings — all listings appear based on location and availability.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Analytics cookies: Set by Google Analytics to track aggregate usage patterns. These cookies do not identify individual users.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These may track your browsing activity across other websites.

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality and will prevent personalized ads from being served, though non-personalized ads may still appear.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites, including business websites, Google Maps listings, and resource sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

FindMyPropane.com is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our site, please contact us so we can remove it.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because we do not sell personal information and collect only minimal data, most CCPA provisions do not apply to our site. If you have questions about your data rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please use our contact form.
