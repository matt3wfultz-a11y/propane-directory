How This Directory Works

Texas Propane Directory lists propane refill and exchange locations across major Texas cities. Each listing includes the business name, address, phone number, available services, and customer ratings. Use the search bar above to find locations by city or business name, or browse by city using the cards above.

Propane suppliers are categorized as Refill (fill your existing tank to capacity), Exchange (swap your empty tank for a pre-filled one), or both. Refill is generally the better value — you pay only for the gas you receive, and your tank is filled to its full rated capacity. Exchange kiosks, commonly found at hardware and grocery stores, are convenient but typically only fill tanks to 75% capacity.
