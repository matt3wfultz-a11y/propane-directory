About Propane Services in Houston

Houston is one of Texas's largest markets for residential and commercial propane use. With a sprawling metro area covering over 670 square miles, residents in neighborhoods like The Woodlands, Sugar Land, Katy, and Pasadena regularly rely on propane for home heating, outdoor cooking, and backup power generation. Many Houston-area propane suppliers offer both tank refill and cylinder exchange services, making it easy to find a location whether you need a standard 20-lb grill tank or a larger residential tank fill.

Propane prices in Houston typically fluctuate with crude oil markets and tend to rise during winter heating season. Calling ahead to confirm current per-gallon pricing before your visit can save time and money.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Houston

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Houston?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its capacity — you pay only for the gas you receive. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one. Exchange tanks are typically only filled to 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity, so refill is usually the better value when a refill station is available nearby.

How much does propane refill cost in Houston?

Propane refill prices in Houston typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon depending on the supplier and current market rates. Prices fluctuate seasonally. Calling ahead or checking the supplier's website is the best way to confirm current pricing before your visit.

Can I get my large propane tank refilled at a Houston location?

Most standard 20-lb (5-gallon) cylinders used for grills can be refilled at any propane refill station. Larger tanks (100 lb, 250-gallon) typically require a scheduled delivery or a trip to a bulk refill facility.
