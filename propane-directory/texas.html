About Propane Services in Texas

Texas is the largest propane-consuming state in the country. With vast rural areas, a booming agricultural sector, and millions of homes using propane for grills, heaters, and whole-home systems, the Lone Star State has one of the most extensive networks of propane dealers and refill stations anywhere in the nation. I tell you what, propane is a big deal around here.

From the Gulf Coast refineries near Houston and Corpus Christi to the high plains of West Texas, propane supply infrastructure runs deep. Major metros like Dallas, San Antonio, Austin, and Fort Worth have dozens of refill stations and exchange kiosks; smaller towns and rural counties are typically served by regional propane dealers who handle delivery and tank installation.

Texas summers push outdoor grillers to burn through propane at a high clip, and the occasional hard freeze — like the ones that hit in 2021 — reminds homeowners why having a reliable propane backup system matters. Whether you need a 20-lb grill cylinder refilled or a 500-gallon residential tank topped off before winter, the directory has you covered.
