About Propane Services in Corpus Christi

Corpus Christi is a coastal Texas city on the Gulf of Mexico, known for its petrochemical industry, tourism, and strong maritime economy. Propane is widely used throughout the Coastal Bend region for residential cooking, outdoor grilling, and heating in the mild but occasionally cold Gulf Coast winters. The city's proximity to the Texas oil and gas industry means propane is generally well-supplied and competitively priced.

Residents of Padre Island, Portland, and Robstown are also served by Corpus Christi-area propane suppliers. Outdoor enthusiasts visiting Padre Island National Seashore frequently stop in Corpus Christi to refill propane cylinders for camping stoves and portable heaters.

Where can I refill my propane tank in Corpus Christi?

Corpus Christi has propane refill and exchange locations at hardware stores, marinas, and dedicated propane suppliers throughout the city and surrounding Nueces County. Use the listings above to find the nearest location with current contact information.

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Corpus Christi?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity — you pay only for what you use. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled one, but exchange tanks are typically filled to only 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is usually the better value when available.

How much does propane cost in Corpus Christi?

Propane prices in Corpus Christi generally range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon. Being close to Gulf Coast refining operations can mean slightly lower prices than inland cities. Prices vary by supplier — call ahead to confirm current rates.
