Dallas Propane Locations

About Propane Services in Dallas

The Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex is home to hundreds of propane suppliers serving residential customers, restaurants, construction sites, and agricultural users across the region. Dallas neighborhoods including Uptown, Oak Cliff, Garland, and Irving all have accessible propane refill and exchange locations. Whether you're filling a grill tank for a backyard barbecue or need regular propane delivery for home heating, the DFW area has reliable options.

Propane exchange kiosks are widely available at hardware stores and grocery chains throughout Dallas. For better per-gallon value, dedicated refill stations fill tanks to full capacity rather than the partial fills common at exchange kiosks.

Frequently Asked Questions – Propane in Dallas

What is the difference between propane refill and exchange in Dallas?

A propane refill fills your existing tank to its full capacity, and you only pay for the gas added. A propane exchange swaps your empty tank for a pre-filled replacement, but exchange tanks are typically filled to only 15 lbs rather than the full 20-lb capacity. Refill is usually the more economical choice when a refill station is nearby.

How much does propane cost in Dallas?

Propane prices in Dallas typically range from $3.00 to $4.50 per gallon for retail refills, though prices fluctuate with the energy market and time of year. Winter months often see higher prices due to heating demand. Always call ahead to get the current per-gallon rate before driving to a location.

Where can I exchange a propane tank in Dallas?

Propane exchange tanks (Blue Rhino, AmeriGas) are available at most major hardware stores, home improvement retailers, and grocery stores across the Dallas metro. Listings marked "Exchange" on this page offer this service. No appointment is needed — simply bring your empty tank and pick up a pre-filled one.

Can I get large propane tanks filled in Dallas?

Yes. Several Dallas-area suppliers can fill larger tanks (100 lb and above) at their retail locations or through scheduled delivery services. Contact the supplier directly to confirm tank size limits and whether an appointment is required for large fills.
